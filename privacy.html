Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you contact us or request Services, such as your name, email address, phone number, property address and any notes relevant to scheduling.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to communicate with you, schedule and deliver our Services, invoice for completed work and maintain basic business records. We may also use aggregated, non‑identifiable data to improve our offerings.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers or subcontractors only as necessary to deliver the Services (for example, scheduling assistants or editors) or as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Photography and media files may be archived for backup and future reference.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at privacy@hommati.com. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

Contact

For any privacy‑related questions or concerns, please email privacy@hommati.com or call (219) 777‑2230.
